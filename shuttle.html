Before the wedding

The trip from the hotel to the DIA is 15-30 minutes depending on traffic. Pick-up at the hotel is at the Michigan Ave entrance. The shuttles run continuously so the times given are estimates. The wedding is from 6 p.m. until midnight.

  • 4:15 PM: Shuttle #1 leaves the Westin Book Cadillac (starts loading at 4PM)
  • 4:30 PM: Shuttle #2 leaves the Westin Book Cadillac
  • 5:15 PM: Shuttle #1 leaves the Westin Book Cadillac
  • 5:30 PM: Shuttle #2 leaves the Westin Book Cadillac
  • 6:00 PM: Shuttle service ends

After the wedding

Each shuttle will make a drop at a bar in Greektown called “The Firebird” before going back to the hotel. The shuttle can’t stop in front of the bar so it will stop at the intersection of Beaubien and Monroe Streets nearby. Guests can get off there or continue on to the hotel. Each trip will take about 30 minutes depending on traffic.

  • 11:15 PM: Shuttle #1 leaves the DIA (starts loading at 11PM)
  • 11:45 PM: Shuttle #2 leaves the DIA
  • 12:15 AM: Shuttle #1 leaves the DIA
  • 12:30 AM: Shuttle #2 leaves the DIA
  • 1:00 AM: Shuttle service ends
